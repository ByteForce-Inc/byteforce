// about us

Built on curiosity.
Powered by code.

ByteForce started when a group of us met during a hackathon weekend. The competition ended but our collaboration didn't. We decided to keep the team alive - as a GitHub org, a community, a family and a space to build ambitious things together.

We're engineers from different backgrounds: frontend, backend i.e microservices, mobile and machine-learning united by a shared drive to ship real projects and sharpen our craft in public.
